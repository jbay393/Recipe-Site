Marmalade French Toast Sandwiches

Who says you can't eat a morning meal at night? When you are hungry in a hurry, this speedy meal gets the job done deliciously.

Ingredints

  • 1 container (8 oz.) whipped cream cheese
  • 12 slices sourdough bread
  • 3/4 cup orange marmalade
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 Tbsp. 2% milk
  • Maple syrup (Optional)

Steps

  1. Spread cream cheese over six slices of bread, top with marmalade and remaining bread.
  2. In a shallow bowl, whisk eggs and milk.
  3. Lightly brease a griddle; heat over medium heat. Dip both sides of sanwhich into egg mixture. Place sandwhich on griddle; toast 2-3 minutes on each side or until golden brown. If desired, serve with syrup.
